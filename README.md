# New-Covenant-of-Faith-Ministries Website
Live Website: https://dree0313.github.io/New-Covenant-of-Faith-Ministries/

New Covenant of Faith Ministries is a welcoming, Spirit-led church committed to teaching the Word of God with clarity, compassion, and conviction. We strive to build strong families, strengthen our community, and help individuals grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ. We believe in worship, fellowship, and walking in God's purpose.

# Client Requirements
About Section
An “About Us” section describing the church’s mission and vision.

Text content including:
Mission statement
Vision statement for 2026

Pastors Section
A “Meet Our Pastors” section with a horizontally scrollable carousel (pastor-scroll) of pastor cards.
Each pastor card contains:
Photo
Name
Title (e.g., Lead Pastor, Co-Pastor)
Short description

First Lady Section
A simple section introducing the First Lady.

Upcoming Events
A React-powered dynamic event listing that displays upcoming events in a scrollable card format.
Each event card includes:
Event title
Date & time
Location

Contact Section
Contact information including email and physical address.

Responsive Design
Mobile adjustments for smaller screens (height changes for overlay, logo resizing, font adjustments).
Interactivity
Hamburger menu that appears after the overlay.
